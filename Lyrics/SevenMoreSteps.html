Seven More Steps

1.
Seven more steps
Push onward thru the mocking cold
A solid wall of rock at my right hand
A hundred feet below me
The river roars and rumbles
It's death by any other name

2.
Seven more steps
Til I walk thru that shadowy door
If I could only push on to redemption
Six more steps until I find sweet surcease
Faith faith I feel the end is near

3.
Seven miles ahead
Where the river meets the plain
Will you really leave your world to be beside me
Six more steps
until I lay my body down
Faith, faith I feel the end is near.
